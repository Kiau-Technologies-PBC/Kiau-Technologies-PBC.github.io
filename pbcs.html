Our Commitment as a Public Benefit Corporation

Kiau Technologies is a Delaware Public Benefit Corporation (PBC): a legal structure that lets us deliver environmental and social impact while pursuing sustainable financial growth.

Unlike traditional corporations, which prioritize returns to shareholders above all else, or nonprofits, which heavily rely on charitable donations, our PBC status allows us to build a self-supporting business that balances the interests of our soils, our partner farms, our communities, our shareholders and our company, and our planet.

What a PBC means for us

Our charter commits us to advancing environmental resilience through developing tools that help farmers regenerate soil health, reduce waste, and strengthen local food systems.

By choosing the PBC model, we're flipping the script on how tech companies operate. We're exploring non-traditional, mission-aligned business models that put long-term ecological health and community benefit at the center.

Our aim is to create tools that not only serve farmers directly but also improve the transparency, longevity, and accessibility of the local food supply.

Who we serve

  • Our soils
  • Our partner farms
  • Our communities
  • Our shareholders and company
  • Our planet

How we deliver benefit

We provide benefit to the public by empowering others to work with the land, not against it.

  1. Design tools that help farmers regenerate soil health and reduce waste.
  2. Keep solutions transparent, durable, and accessible to local food systems.
  3. Balance ecological impact with sustainable financial growth so the mission endures.
