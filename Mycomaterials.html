The Benefits of Mycomaterials

  • Biodegradability: Mycomaterials are fully biodegradable (in your backyard, not in a commercial composting facility) in under 90 days.
  • Low Embodied Carbon: Flaunts low embodied carbon, and can be desiccated without added energy.
  • Water Proof: Mycomaterials create semi-waterproof surface layers.
  • Fire Proofing: Mycomaterials have excellent fire resistance properties, making them suitable for use in a variety of applications.
